Why is web presence important?

A strong online presence allows you to build your brand and gain the credibility that you need to attract more customers. Along with making your business readily accessible, online presence also gives your customers an easy way to find out more about what you have to offer.

    • A website provides ease of information access for your organization and serve as a hub (all in one, one-stop shop). Used for marketing, advertising, and branding purposes, can have capabilities to create organizational systems such as financial system, marketing, and other information systems.

  • Graphic Design

    • Graphic designers create visual concepts, using computer software or by hand, to communicate ideas that inspire, inform, and captivate consumers. They develop the overall layout and production design for applications such as advertisements, brochures, magazines, and reports.

  • Social Media Management

    • Social media can help your business to attract customers, get customer feedback, and build customer loyalty. It can increase your market reach, including international markets, Be able to exchange ideas to improve the way you do business. Data Intelligence vs. Data Analytics

Data intelligence collects and inspects data about actions, events, and other information to determine what happened in the past and why. Data analytics uses the same data from data intelligence and applies data science and analytics techniques to predict what will happen in the future, then make business decisions based on that data.

  • Data Intelligence

    • Data intelligence collects and inspects data about actions, events, and other information to determine what happened in the past and why.

  • Data Analytics

    • Data analytics uses the same data from data intelligence and applies data science and analytics techniques to predict what will happen in the future, then make business decisions based on that data.
