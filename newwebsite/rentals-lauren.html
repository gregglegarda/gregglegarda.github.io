The Lauren Condominiums

Dupont Circle, Washington, D.C.

  • 1301 20th St. North West, Washington DC 20036

The Lauren is a high-rise condominium at the corner of 20th and N Streets NW in the Dupont Circle neighborhood of Washington, DC. Amenities include a rooftop deck with swimming pool and a 24-hour front desk. The 10-story building was constructed in 1973 with 168 studio, one-bedroom, and two-bedroom residences.
