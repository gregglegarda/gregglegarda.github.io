Intro

Here is a little background on my education:
I am a George Washington University Alumni and I have acquired degrees in Associate of Science in Health Science in Medical Laboratory Technology (ASCP certified) - Class of 2013, Bachelor of Business Administration w/ Concentration in Information Systems Technology Management and Minor in Computer Science - Class of 2019, and a Master of Science in Information Systems Technology w/ Graduate Certificate in Data Science - Class of 2020.

My work experience include:
Working as kitchen staff, line cook, server and cashier at TA Country Pride Restaurant in Marianna Florida from 2009 to 2010. Then I decided to join and became a member of The United States Navy as a Hospital Corpsman for 5 years from 2010 to 2015. During that time, I was stationed in Great Lakes Illinois, Fort Sam Houston Texas, and San Diego California. I also worked as a Medical Laboratory Technician and a Cytologist Assistant in Captain James A.
