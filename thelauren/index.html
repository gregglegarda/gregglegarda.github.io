About the Lauren

The Lauren is a high-rise condominium at the corner of 20th and N Streets NW in the Dupont Circle neighborhood of Washington, DC. Amenities include a rooftop deck with swimming pool and a 24-hour front desk. The 10-story building was constructed in 1973 with 168 studio, one-bedroom, and two-bedroom residences and indoor and exterior parking.

The Lauren provides its residents with an exceptional combination of location, convenience, and security. The building is a 4-minute walk from the Dupont Circle Metro Station. Dupont Circle's shops and restaurants are nearby, and Georgetown and Adams Morgan are within a short walk..
