Why is online presence important?

A strong online presence allows you to build your brand and gain the credibility that you need to attract more customers. Along with making your business readily accessible, online presence also gives your customers an easy way to find out more about what you have to offer.

All About Data

Data Intelligence vs. Data Analytics

Data intelligence collects and inspects data about actions, events, and other information to determine what happened in the past and why. Data analytics uses the same data from data intelligence and applies data science and analytics techniques to predict what will happen in the future, then make business decisions based on that data.
