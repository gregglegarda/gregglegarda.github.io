Bristlemor

Kenmanor Condominium

  • 3355 University Blvd, Kensington, Maryland 20895

Welcome to Bristlemor, a stunning and modern second bedroom located in the Kenmanor Condominium building in the heart of Kensington, MD. This beautiful room is in a unit situated on the second floor top-corner and offers convenient and comfortable living for anyone seeking a modern lifestyle.

The unit is fully equipped with all the necessary amenities to make your stay comfortable, including heating and air conditioning, modern kitchen and bathrooms, and high-speed internet with speeds up to 300mbps. The unit is also beautifully furnished with stylish decor and comfortable furniture, making it the perfect place to relax and unwind.

Rent includes water, electric, heat, cool, and gas, making it easier for you to manage your expenses. The Kenmanor Condominiums are located in a prime location, with easy access to public transportation. The building is situated near Bus 34 that goes to Navy Medical Center Metro Red Line, making it easy to get around and explore the surrounding area.

The Kenmanor Condominium building comprises 25 units and is conveniently located just a few minutes away from shopping at the Westfield Mall, the MARC train station, and the Wheaton Metro Red Line. Bristlemor is the perfect choice for anyone seeking a modern and convenient lifestyle.

This room is perfect for active duty military members or single professionals and work from home professionals. The bedroom is equipped with a comfortable bed and plenty of storage space, making it easy to settle in and feel right at home. The 1.5 bathrooms are recently refreshed, and a new HVAC system is installed.

Overall, Bristlemor offers modern and convenient living in a prime location. With its convenient amenities, nearby access to public transportation, and beautiful design, Bristlemor is the ideal choice for anyone seeking a comfortable and convenient lifestyle.
