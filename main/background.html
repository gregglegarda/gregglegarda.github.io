Biography

Gregg Legarda is a Filipino-American who has had a diverse and accomplished career in a range of industries. He was born in San Fernando, Pampanga in the Philippines and grew up in Daraga, Albay. Growing up in the Philippines, Gregg was raised by his mother's sister's family while his mother worked in the United States as a nurse to support him financially. This likely meant that he had to live with a non-traditional family unit and may have faced challenges related to this arrangement. When he moved to the United States in his late teens, Gregg lived independently in a small trailer in a park and supported himself by working as a server in a restaurant. This required a significant level of independence and self-reliance at a young age. Additionally, Gregg faced the challenges of military life as a member of the United States Navy. This likely included physical and mental training, as well as the demands of serving in the military and potentially being stationed to different locations. Overall, Gregg has faced and overcome a number of challenges throughout his life, demonstrating resilience and determination in the face of adversity.

Gregg Legarda received his primary education at the Bicol University College of Education Laboratory School and attended Sunshine International Learning School before elementary school. He completed his secondary education at Daraga National High School and Bicol College High School, where he received his diploma. After high school, Gregg enrolled in nursing school at Bicol University Tabaco Campus, completing a ladderized program in midwifery and nursing. After moving to the United States, Gregg attended Chipola University in Marianna, Florida and took general education courses. He later joined the United States Navy and attended Corps School, NSHS, and METC. He earned an Associates Degree from George Washington University in Washington D.C. with a certificate in Medical Laboratory Technology and an ASCP certificate. He then went on to earn a Bachelors in Business Administration in Information Systems Technology Management with Computer Science from GWU. Finally, Gregg earned a Masters in Information Systems Technology with Data Science from GWU. Throughout his education, Gregg has also obtained various certifications to sharpen his skills and knowledge in his fields of interest.

Gregg Legarda has had a diverse professional career, holding a range of positions in various industries. After moving to the United States and completing his general education courses at Chipola University, Gregg worked as a dishwasher, server, cashier, and cook at the Country Pride Restaurant in the Travel Centers of America in Marianna, Florida. He later joined the United States Navy as a Hospital Corpsman and specialized in Medical Laboratory Technology.
