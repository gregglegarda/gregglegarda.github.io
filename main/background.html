Gregg Legarda

BUSINESS AND TECHNOLOGY SYSTEMS STRATEGIST. FOCUSED ON AUTOMATION, EFFICIENCY, AND QUALITY.

  • Based in Washington, D.C.

Information Systems Technology & Data Professional

Gregg Legarda is a distinguished information systems technology professional whose expertise spans data science, healthcare technology, and military service. His unique career path, bridging healthcare and technology, has been shaped by an exceptional educational background and diverse professional experiences.

Professional Excellence

As a Senior Data Pipeline Engineer at Quality Business Engineering (QBE), Legarda supports HQDA G-3/5/7, DAMO-SO for the Civilian Harm Mitigation and Response, where he plays a key role in establishing data foundations for the CHMR platform. At ALKU Government Solutions, he served as a Data Engineer for the CDAO TSYBO DLA Mercury Team in the ADVANA Platform, furthering his expertise in military data systems. His role as a Data Scientist and Research Scientist at Teledyne Brown Engineering provided crucial support to the U.S. Navy Bureau of Medicine and Surgery, leveraging advanced analytics to enhance military healthcare operations. Previously, as a Scientific Programmer and Data Visualization Developer at Ace Info, supporting the National Oceanic Atmospheric Administration (NOAA), he demonstrated his ability to translate complex data into actionable insights through innovative visualization tools. Legarda's foundation in healthcare, established during his service as a Hospital Corpsman in the U.S. Navy specializing in Medical Laboratory Technology, gives him a unique perspective in bridging the gap between healthcare operations and technological solutions. This distinctive combination of medical and technical expertise has proven invaluable in his approach to systems design and process optimization.

Academic Achievement

Legarda's commitment to excellence is reflected in his academic achievements: - Master of Science in Information Systems Technology with a focus on Data Science from George Washington University - Bachelor of Business Administration in Information Systems Technology Management with a Computer Science focus from George Washington University - Associate Degree in Medical Laboratory Technology from George Washington University, complemented by ASCP certification

Entrepreneurial Spirit

Beyond his technical expertise, Legarda has demonstrated business acumen through successful ventures in real estate investment, managing multiple properties and developing additional revenue streams through strategic property management.

International Background

Born in San Fernando, Pampanga, Philippines, and educated at prestigious institutions including Bicol University, Legarda's international perspective enriches his approach to problem-solving and innovation. His early education in nursing and midwifery at Bicol University Tabaco Campus laid the groundwork for his later specialization in healthcare technology.

Professional Impact

Legarda's career exemplifies the power of combining domain expertise with technical innovation. His work consistently demonstrates the ability to: - Bridge the gap between healthcare operations and technological solutions - Develop data-driven insights that inform strategic decision-making - Create efficient systems that streamline complex processes - Apply cross-disciplinary knowledge to solve challenging problems Today, Gregg Legarda continues to push the boundaries of information systems technology, bringing his unique blend of healthcare knowledge, technical expertise, and innovative thinking to complex challenges in both public and private sectors.
