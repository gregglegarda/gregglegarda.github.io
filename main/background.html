Gregg Legarda

BUSINESS AND TECHNOLOGY SYSTEMS STRATEGIST. FOCUSED ON AUTOMATION, EFFICIENCY, AND QUALITY.

  • Based in Washington, D.C.

Gregg Legarda is an information systems technology professional with a unique background in the US Navy and in Healthcare, as well as excellent skills in a variety of technical languages and platforms, analysis, and systems design. After learning about health science and how medical laboratories functioned, directed attention to developing easier and more efficient processes through technology and systems. Strengthened knowledge and abilities in data science and computer science for a solid foundation. Earned a degree of Master of Science in Information Systems Technology with Data Science, and Bachelor of Business Administration in Information Systems Technology Management with Computer Science.

Biography

Gregg Legarda was born in San Fernando, Pampanga, Philippines. He grew up in Daraga, Albay, Philippines with his mother's sister's family. His aunt, uncle and cousins raised him up and served as his family while his mother worked in the United States as a nurse to provide him financial support for his studies in the Philippines. His mother took him to the United States during his late teens to live with them in Marianna, Florida. After about a year, he moved out of his mother's home to a small trailer in a park and lived independently for the rest of his late teens. He work as a server in a restaurant to support himself while also taking general education courses at a college. He then moved to Great Lakes, Illinois for bootcamp in the United States Navy. He was stationed in Fort Sam Houston Texas, San Diego California, and Great Lakes / North Chicago Illinois. He moved to Washington DC to pursue business degrees in GWU. He stayed in the DMV area after he graduated, for his professional career.

He attended Sunshine International Learning School before elementary school. His primary school is Bicol University College of Education Laboratory School. His secondary schools are Daraga National High School and Bicol College High School where he graduated and got his diploma. He then went to Bicol University Tabaco Campus for Nursing School with a ladderized program of midwife and nursing. He attended Chipola University in the United States in Marianna, Florida to take some more general education. He joined the US Navy and attended Corps School, NSHS, and METC. He got an Associates Degree from George Washington University with MLT Certificate and ASCP Certificate. He went to George Washington University in Washington D.C to pursue an undergraduate business degree in Bachelors in Business Administration in Information Systems Technology Management with Computer Science. For his graduate degree, he earned a Masters in Information Systems Technology with Data Science, He continues to get certifications to sharpen his skills and knowledge about his fields of interests.

He worked as a dishwasher, server, cashier and cook at the Country Pride Restaurant in the Travel Centers of America in Marianna Florida. This is while he was taking some general education courses at Chipola University. He joined the US Navy with an introduction of a friend that he met through his job in the restaurant at a Delayed Entry Program (DEP) in Panama City Florida. He enlisted as a Hospital Corpsman and specialized in Medial Laboratory Technology. After he graduated from GWU, he worked as a Scientific Programmer and Data Visualization Developer for Ace Info under National Oceanic Atmospheric Administration (NOAA). He also worked as a Data Scientist and Research Scientist for Teledyne Brown Engineering under United States Navy Bureau of Medicine and Surgery.
