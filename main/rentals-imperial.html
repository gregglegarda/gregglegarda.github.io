Imperial House

Dupont Circle, Washington, D.C.

  • 1601 18th St. North West, Washington DC 20009

Imperial House is located in the Dupont Circle neighborhood at the corner of 18th Street NW and Q Street. This location offers a WalkScore of 97 and is within walking distance of the Dupont Circle Metro Station. Cosmopolitan dining, boutique shopping and fitness and yoga studios are located close by. This contemporary condo development is in close proximity to many embassies. Delivered in 1962 by Landow & Brandt, the 10-story building offers 162 residences with city views. Amenities at Imperial House include a 24-hour front desk, rooftop deck with sweeping views of the city, yoga/meditation room, common laundry & bike storage.
